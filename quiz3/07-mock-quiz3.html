Problem 1 · Sampling & Definitions ~6 分钟

1.1 Suppose students is a DataFrame containing every student at UCSD (the entire population). Which of the following code lines produces a Simple Random Sample (SRS) of 50 students? Select all that apply.

  • (A) students.take(np.arange(50))
  • (B) students.sample(50)
  • (C) students.sample(50, replace=True)
  • (D) np.random.choice(students.get('name'), 50, replace=False)
  • (E) np.random.choice(students.get('name'), 50)

1.2 True / False:

  • (a) Parameters are fixed; statistics vary across samples.
  • (b) Larger SRS sizes lead to better estimates of parameters.
  • (c) The bootstrap distribution of the sample mean approximates the population distribution.
显示答案 & 解析

1.1: (B)。

  • (A) ❌ take(np.arange(50)) 取前 50 行，不随机。
  • (B) ✅ .sample(50) 默认 replace=False，标准 SRS。
  • (C) ❌ replace=True，不是 SRS。
  • (D) ⚠️ Tricky：np.random.choice 在列上采样（只拿 name），不是采 row。即使 replace=False，丢失了其他列。严格按 DSC10 题目意图通常视为不是。
  • (E) ❌ np.random.choice 默认 replace=True。

1.2: (a) ✅ True / (b) ✅ True / (c) ❌ False — 是 sampling distribution of sample mean，不是 population values 分布。

Problem 2 · Bootstrap & CI Construction ~10 分钟

UCSD 食堂调研。food 是一个 DataFrame，每行是一份 entrée，含 price (float)、category (str)。你从 food 抽了一个 SRS of 200 entrées 叫 my_sample。

2.1 Fill in: my_sample = food.sample(__(a)__)　（要 SRS）

2.2 Fill in the bootstrap code that computes a 10000-resample distribution of the mean price of vegan entrées:

vegan = __(b)__
boot_means = np.array([])
for i in np.arange(10000):
    resample = vegan.sample(__(c)__, replace=__(d)__)
    boot_means = np.append(boot_means, __(e)__)

2.3 Fill in for an 88% bootstrap CI:

left  = np.percentile(boot_means, __(f)__)
right = np.percentile(boot_means, __(g)__)

2.4 The distribution of boot_means approximates ____. (Pick one)

  • (A) the population distribution of entrée prices
  • (B) the sampling distribution of the sample mean
  • (C) the empirical distribution of vegan prices
  • (D) a parameter
显示答案 & 解析

2.1 (a): 200（默认 replace=False = SRS）

2.2 (b): my_sample[my_sample.get('category') == 'vegan']

2.2 (c): vegan.shape[0]（resample size 等于 subset 大小，不是 200）

2.2 (d): True

2.2 (e): resample.get('price').mean()

2.3 (f): 6　(g): 94（88% → 两端各砍 6%）

2.4: (B)。Bootstrap 分布近似 sampling distribution of the statistic (mean)。

Problem 3 · CI Interpretation ~5 分钟

承前 2.3，假设 CI 算出来是 [12.50, 15.20]. Select all valid interpretations:

  • (A) There is an 88% probability that the population mean is in [12.50, 15.20].
  • (B) 88% of vegan entrées at UCSD have a price between $12.50 and $15.20.
  • (C) About 88% of the 10000 bootstrap means fell between $12.50 and $15.20.
  • (D) If we repeated the sample-then-bootstrap-then-CI procedure many times, ~88% of the resulting intervals would contain the population mean.
  • (E) The sample mean of vegan prices in my_sample is approximately in [12.50, 15.20].
显示答案 & 解析

答案：(C), (D), (E)

  • (A) ❌ "X% probability"陷阱。Parameter 固定，interval 固定，没有概率。
  • (B) ❌ "X% of values"陷阱。CI 描述 parameter，不描述个体值。
  • (C) ✅ 这就是 88% CI 的构造方式。
  • (D) ✅ Confidence is in the process. 正确解读。
  • (E) ✅ Sample mean 通常落在 boot 分布中心，CI 一般会包含它。
Problem 4 · Chebyshev & Normal ~6 分钟

The amount of time (in minutes) students study per week has mean 20 and SD 6.

4.1 Find the smallest interval guaranteed to contain at least 88.88% of the data. Both endpoints integers.

4.2 If the data is normally distributed, approximately what % of data is in the interval from 4.1?

4.3 Still assuming normal: find x in:

prop = stats.norm.cdf(x) - stats.norm.cdf(0)

so that prop ≈ proportion of students studying between 20 and 26 minutes.

显示答案 & 解析

4.1: 88.88% = 8/9 → 1/z² = 1/9 → z = 3。区间 = 20 ± 3·6 = [2, 38]。

4.2: z=3 在 normal 下 ≈ 99.73%。

4.3: 标准化两个端点： x = (26−20)/6 = 1，0 = (20−20)/6。所以 x = 1。

Problem 5 · CLT-based CI + Reverse-Engineering ~10 分钟

You collect a sample of n = 100 students. Sample mean study time = 22 min, sample SD = 5 min.

5.1 Compute a 95% CLT-based CI for the population mean.

5.2 If you instead collect n = 25 students with the same sample mean and SD, what's the new 95% CI?

5.3 You're shown a 95% CI = [18, 26] for some other dataset's population mean. The sample size was n = 9. What was the sample SD?

5.4 Given stats.norm.cdf(1.28) = 0.90, construct an 80% CLT-based CI for the original sample (n=100, mean=22, SD=5).

显示答案 & 解析

5.1: SE = 5/√100 = 0.5。CI = [22 − 2·0.5, 22 + 2·0.5] = [21, 23]。

5.2: SE = 5/√25 = 1。CI = [22 − 2, 22 + 2] = [20, 24]。（n 从 100 → 25，n 变 1/4 → 宽度变 2 倍）

5.3: Width = 26 − 18 = 8。Half-width = 4 = 2·SD/√9 = 2·SD/3 → SD = 6。

5.4: z 满足 cdf(z) = (1+0.80)/2 = 0.90 → z = 1.28。half-width = 1.28 · 0.5 = 0.64。CI = [22 − 0.64, 22 + 0.64] = [21.36, 22.64]。

Problem 6 · Sample Size for Polling ~5 分钟

You want to estimate the proportion of UCSD students who own a bike. You want a 95% CI of width at most 0.04.

6.1 What is the minimum sample size required?

6.2 If you change the requirement to width ≤ 0.02 (halved), how many additional people must you sample beyond 6.1?

显示答案 & 解析

6.1: n ≥ (4·0.5/0.04)² = (50)² = 2500。n = 2500。

6.2: n ≥ (4·0.5/0.02)² = (100)² = 10000。多采 10000 − 2500 = 7500 人。（w 减半 → n 变 4 倍）

W26 Problem 1 · Distribution Properties (Chebyshev / CLT / Skew)

(a) We expect at least 75% of all sampled values of plays_minchan lie within 2 SDs of the population mean.

  • Yes, due to CLT　/　Yes, due to Chebyshev's　/　No, due to CLT　/　No, due to Chebyshev's　/　Yes, other reasons　/　No, other reasons

(b) We expect approximately 95% of all sample means lie within 2 SDs of the population mean.

  • (same options)

(c) If the distribution of plays_minchan is right-skewed but with no outliers, which is most likely true?

  • median > mean　/　mean > median　/　mean = median

(d) If both Minchan's and Ray's plays distributions are normal, will the pooled distribution be approximately normal?

  • Always　/　Sometimes　/　Never
显示答案 & 解析

(a) Yes, due to Chebyshev's。Chebyshev: ≥ 1 − 1/2² = 75% within 2 SDs，对任何分布成立。

(b) Yes, due to CLT（⚠️ 措辞陷阱 — 官方答案）。CLT → sample means 分布近似 normal；该分布的 SD = SE。题目"within 2 standard deviations of the population mean"中"SDs"按官方解读 = sample means 分布的 SD（即 SE），所以由 CLT 直接给 95%。许多人会误答 No（把 SDs 当成 population SD）。详细两种解读对照见 Section 4 此题完整解析。

(c) mean > median。右偏 → mean 跟着 tail 拉到右边。

(d) Sometimes。两个 normal mix 后只有 mean、SD 相同时才仍 normal；否则可能 bimodal。

W26 Problem 2 · SRS Identification

Select all sampling methods that give a Simple Random Sample (SRS) of 20 songs from songs:

  • (A) songs.sample(20, replace=1)
  • (B) np.random.multinomial(20, np.ones(songs.shape[0]) / songs.shape[0])
  • (C) np.random.choice(songs.get("song_name"), 20)
  • (D) Randomly pick 20 genres without replacement, then within each genre pick a song.
  • (E) Randomly pick 20 artists without replacement, then within each artist pick a song.
  • (F) None of the above.
显示答案 & 解析

(F) None of the above。

  • (A) ❌ replace=1 is truthy = True → 有放回。
  • (B) ❌ multinomial 永远有放回。
  • (C) ❌ choice 默认 replace=True；并且只取了一列。
  • (D)(E) ❌ Stratified sampling，每首歌概率与 genre/artist 大小相关。
W26 Problem 3 · Bootstrap & CI

Ray collected an SRS of his whole playlist (unspecified size) → ray_playlist. Bootstrap to construct an 80% CI for the population mean of plays:

boot_means = np.array([])
for i in range(1000):
    sim = ray_playlist.___(a)___.get("plays_ray").mean()
    boot_means = np.append(boot_means, sim)
left = np.percentile(boot_means, ___(b)___)
right = np.percentile(boot_means, ___(c)___)

(d) The distribution of boot_means approximates: (A) sampling distribution of sample mean; (B) population distribution; (C) empirical distribution of plays; (D) probability distribution of plays

(e) Which interpretations of the resulting CI [a, b] are correct?

  • (A) 80% of songs have plays in [a, b]
  • (B) 80% probability the true mean is in [a, b]
  • (C) 80% of such intervals from repeating the process will contain the true mean
  • (D) 80% of the bootstrap means lie in [a, b]
显示答案 & 解析

(a) sample(ray_playlist.shape[0], replace=True)

(b) 10　(c) 90（80% → (100−80)/2 = 10）

(d) (A) sampling distribution of sample mean。

(e) (C) 是 frequentist 正解。(D) 字面上也对（构造方式），常作可选。课程标准答案通常只取 (C)。

W26 Problem 4 · CLT-based CI (反推 + 非标准置信度)

Ray calculated a 95% CLT-based CI for the population mean of plays per song: [60.5, 100.5]. Sample size = 25 songs.

(a) What is the SD of Ray's plays?

(b) If Ray increases the sample size to 100, how does the CI width change?

(c) Given stats.norm.cdf(1.75) = 0.96, what is the 92% CLT-based CI?

(d) Ray constructs a 99% CI from the same data. Compared to the 95% CI, is the 99% CI wider, narrower, or same?

显示答案 & 解析

(a) Width = 40, half-width = 20 = 2·SD/√25 = 2·SD/5 → SD = 50。

(b) n × 4 → width × 1/2 → halves。

(c) z = 1.75 (因为 cdf(z) = (1+0.92)/2 = 0.96)。SE = 50/√25 = 10。half-width = 1.75·10 = 17.5。Sample mean = (60.5+100.5)/2 = 80.5。CI = [63.0, 98.0]。

(d) Wider（更高置信 → 更宽）。

🎯 高频陷阱题速刷（25 道 T/F + 选择）

这 25 道每个都对应一个高频陷阱。2 秒内回答 + 1 秒内说出原因。错的标记下来重点复习。

  1. SRS 必须 without replacement。
    显示答案True（DSC10 严格定义）
  2. df.sample(n) 默认 with replacement。
    显示答案False。默认 replace=False。np.random.choice 才默认 True。
  3. np.random.multinomial 是 SRS。
    显示答案False。永远有放回。
  4. Parameter 随样本变化。
    显示答案False。Parameter 是 population 的固定数。
  5. Sampling distribution of statistic 的 spread 与样本量无关。
    显示答案False。n ↑ → spread ↓（√n 反比）。
  6. Bootstrap 是从 sample 还是 population 中采样？
    显示答案Sample（关键定义）。从 population 采叫普通 sampling，不叫 bootstrap。
  7. Bootstrap 必须 with replacement。
    显示答案True。否则每个 resample 都是原 sample 的 permutation。
  8. Bootstrap resample 的 size 应当等于 population size。
    显示答案False。应等于原 sample size。
  9. 90% CI 的 percentile 是 10 和 90。
    显示答案False。是 5 和 95。 (100−90)/2 = 5。
  10. 80% CI 的 percentile 是 10 和 90。
    显示答案True。 (100−80)/2 = 10。
  11. "95% chance the true mean is in [a, b]" 是 95% CI 的正确解读？
    显示答案False。Parameter 固定，interval 固定，没有概率。
  12. "95% of sample values lie in this CI"？
    显示答案False。CI 描述 parameter，不描述 data values。
  13. 99% CI 比 95% CI 窄。
    显示答案False。更高置信 → 更宽。
  14. Bootstrap 可用来估 population max。
    显示答案False。Resample max ≤ 原 sample max，永远低估。
  15. Right-skewed → mean > median。
    显示答案True。"Mean follows tail."
  16. SD 可以为负数。
    显示答案False。SD ≥ 0。
  17. SD 可以为 0。
    显示答案True。当所有值相等。
  18. Sum of deviations from mean = 0。
    显示答案True。Mean 的定义性质。
  19. Chebyshev: mean ± 2 SD 至少包含 75%。
    显示答案True。1 − 1/4 = 0.75。
  20. Chebyshev 告诉我们 mean ± 1 SD 至少包含 50%。
    显示答案False。1 − 1/1 = 0 → 至少 0%（无意义）。
  21. Normal 分布下 ±2 SD 约包含 95%。
    显示答案True。68/95/99.7 法则。
  22. Standardization 把分布变成 normal。
    显示答案False。只是平移 + 缩放，分布形状不变。
  23. CLT 要求 population 是 normal。
    显示答案False。CLT 对任何 population 成立。
  24. CLT 也适用于 sample median。
    显示答案False。CLT 只适用于 mean 和 sum。
  25. SD of 0s and 1s 的最大值是 1。
    显示答案False。是 0.5（at p=0.5）。

错题分析模板

对每道错题，回答以下 3 个问题：

  1. 错的是 concept 还是 calculation？
    • Concept 错 → 回 Section 的"知识点"
    • Calculation 错 → 抄一遍正确步骤
  2. 这是哪个陷阱？给它一个名字（如"replace 默认值反"、"X% chance 陷阱"、"sqrt law 用反"）。
  3. 把对应 Section 的 checklist 项重做一次。

考前最后 24 小时

必背清单（默写）：
  1. Bootstrap loop 模板（含 replace=True、.shape[0]）
  2. c% CI 的 percentile：(100−c)/2 和 (100+c)/2
  3. 95% CLT CI 公式：mean ± 2·SD/√n
  4. z 通用公式：cdf(z) = (1+c)/2
  5. Chebyshev：1 − 1/z²；z=2/3/4 → 75/88.8/93.75%
  6. Normal 68/95/99.7 法则
  7. Sample size 公式：n ≥ (4·SD/w)²；SD of 0/1 max = 0.5
  8. CI 解读: confidence in process，~X% of intervals
考试当场易错三件：
  1. 读 replace 默认值有没有看对（choice True / sample False）
  2. 百分位算错（80% 是 10/90 不是 20/80）
  3. SE 和 SD 混淆：CLT-based CI 用的是 SE = SD/√n，不是 SD 本身
